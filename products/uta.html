  • UTA
    • UTA is a new generation agricultural research platform that can be used to deliver tasks like seeding, weeding, spraying and plant phenotyping autonomously. It is a 4WS/4WD vehicle that is designed from scratch for specifically agricultural applications. The platform can carry agricultural modules up to 500 kg. In order to increase the number of robotic applications in agriculture, we are inviting partners from universities, research institutions and other private companies to design modules for UTA. Get in touch with us if you want to develop or already have a tool that we can mount on UTA.
