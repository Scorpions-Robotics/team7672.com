  • PTK3000
    • PTK3000 is designed to automate your internal transportation of heavy loads. With its 7xxx series aluminum based strong and lightweight chassis, it can carry materials up to 3000 kg. The self driving vehicle operates with natural navigation-based autonomy and have ability to work both indoor and outdoor environments. The lithium-based battery system enables longer operation times and minimizes down time.
